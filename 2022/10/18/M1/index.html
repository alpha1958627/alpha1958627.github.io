Axial deformation

\[\begin{align} \Delta l=\frac{F_Nl}{EA} \end{align}\] \(E\) is the modulus of elasticity
And we call \(EA\) rigidity - Poisson's ratio \[\begin{align} \mu=-\frac{\varepsilon'}{\varepsilon} \end {align}\] \(\varepsilon'\) is Lateral strain
